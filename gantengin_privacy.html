Privacy Policy for Gantengin Last Updated: 9 March 2026 Gantengin ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. 1. Information Collection and Use Gantengin is a camera and image enhancement application. To provide its features, the App requires access to certain functions on your device: Camera: To capture photos and record videos. Microphone: To capture audio during video recording. Storage (Media/Gallery): To save processed photos/videos and allow you to view them within the App. Face Data: The App uses Face Detection (ML Kit) and Image Segmentation to provide focus-locking and bokeh effects. 2. Data Processing (AI & Face Detection) We prioritize your privacy by processing data locally on your device: No Cloud Uploads: All image processing, including AI Enhancement (NCNN) and Face Detection, is performed on-device. We do not upload your photos, videos, or face data to any external servers. Face Data Handling: Face data is used only in real-time to determine focus points and background segmentation. This data is ephemeral and is not stored or shared with third parties. 3. Third-Party Services The App may use third-party libraries to improve performance: Google ML Kit / CameraX: For camera functionality and face detection. WorkManager: To handle background AI processing. These services do not collect personally identifiable information from your use of Gantengin. 4. Permissions The App will ask for the following permissions: android.permission.CAMERA android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (or Media permissions for Android 13+) You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, though it may limit the App's functionality. 5. Security We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of the App. Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, the primary security of your photos remains on your local device. 6. Children’s Privacy Gantengin does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since all processing is local, we do not have a database of any user information. 7. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. 8.
